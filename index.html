Privacy Policy for FitOra

Effective Date: August 8, 2025
Last Updated: August 8, 2025

FitOra ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

- Name, email address, and password (when you sign up or log in using Firebase Authentication).

1.2 Usage Data

- Workout logs and progress information you enter in the app.

1.3 Automatically Collected Data

- Device type, operating system version, and other technical details for app performance and analytics.

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as financial details, government identification numbers, or precise GPS location.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide core app functionality (e.g., saving workouts, unlocking weeks).
  • Maintain and improve the app.
  • Authenticate and manage user accounts.
  • Communicate updates and important information.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely in Google Firebase.
  • Encryption in Transit: All data sent between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Encryption at Rest: Firebase stores your data securely using industry-standard encryption methods.

4. Children’s Privacy (COPPA & Families Policy Compliance)

FitOra is not specifically targeted at children under 13, but if a child uses the app:

  • All content will be appropriate for a general audience.
  • Ads (if shown) will come only from Google Play certified ad networks and will be suitable for all ages.
  • We comply with applicable laws, including COPPA and GDPR, for processing any child’s data.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share limited data only:

  • With service providers (such as Firebase) to operate and maintain the app.
  • When required by law or legal processes.

6. Third-Party Services

The app uses trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication – for secure login and account management.
  • Firebase Firestore – for storing workout logs and user progress.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.
  • Request a copy of your stored data.

You can make such requests by contacting us (see Section 10).

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and updated on our website.

9. Delete My Account & Data

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us (see Section 10)

Use the subject line: **Account Deletion Request** In the body of the email, include: - Your registered email address used in the app - A statement confirming you want your account and data deleted
